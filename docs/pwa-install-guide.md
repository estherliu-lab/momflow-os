# PWA Install Guide / 添加到桌面指南

MomFlow is designed for quick returns. The home-screen icon matters because creators often open the app at the exact moment an idea appears or an interruption happens.

## iPhone

1. Open the deployed MomFlow site in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Choose **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Keep the name as **MomFlow** or change it to **抱抱獭**.
5. Tap **Add**.

## Android

1. Open MomFlow in Chrome or a compatible browser.
2. Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home screen** when prompted.
3. If no prompt appears, open the browser menu and choose **Install app**.

## 中文说明

MomFlow 的核心使用场景是“刚有灵感”和“刚被打断”的瞬间。添加到手机桌面后，用户不用先打开浏览器、找网址、再进入页面，路径会短很多，留存也会更好。
